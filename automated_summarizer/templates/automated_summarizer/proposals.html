    Proposals

    We propose that the way to make people understand the legalese in TOS/EULA/Licenses is to present the legalese in mutliple formats:

    1. Present the terms and conditions in comic format (similar to Chrome's comic or Dvorak Zine). A sample comic representation of first paragraph of Yahoo! India Terms and Conditions is (click to enlarge):

    2. We should present a summary of legalese in natural languages like English in simple English for laymans (E.g. Plain English aka layman version of Google's Terms of Service)

    3. We should present a one-line summary of all major clauses in TOS. (E.g. 500px's Terms of Service or Commons Deed of Creative Commons)

    4. We should invest efforts into an automated agreement summarizer (similar to the one we made for this hackathon) so that anyone should be able to translate gazillions of TOS/EULA/Licenses out there in wild on Internet.
